About Rich Snider

Rich Snider

I had the unique opportunity of being able to work for a couple Mennonite furniture makers here in the rural Waterloo region. These formative years really helped spark my passion for wood and wood projects. I have always loved spending time on the lathe. There is something so visually pleasing as I see a beautiful project being made from a rough piece of lumber.About 2 years ago, I came across a video of a guy turning and making his own pens and was instantly intrigued with the whole process. I made my first pen shortly after and have been hooked ever since. I have always loved working with my hands and creating beautiful wood projects from rough lumber and timber.
